About

Nike is a multinational corporation that is engaged in the design, development, and manufacturing of athletic footwear, apparel, and equipment. The company was founded in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports by Phil Knight and Bill Bowerman, and officially became Nike, Inc. in 1971. Nike is headquartered in Beaverton, Oregon, and operates numerous retail stores around the world, as well as an e-commerce website. The company's iconic "swoosh" logo and "Just Do It" slogan are recognized globally. Nike offers a wide range of products for athletes and fitness enthusiasts, including running, basketball, soccer, and training footwear, as well as sports apparel and equipment. In addition to its core business, Nike also sponsors high-profile athletes and sports teams, and has a strong presence in the world of sports sponsorship and marketing.
